California Poppy

A gentle relaxant historically used to support restful sleep and calm nervous tension.

Evening Primrose

Traditionally used for skin health and hormonal balance; seeds are prized for evening primrose oil.

  • Harvest: Collect seeds when pods dry; press for oil or store seeds.
  • How to use: Topical oil: Use seed oil topically for dry skin or include in DIY balms.

Yarrow

A versatile first-aid herb for minor wounds, inflammation and to support circulation.

  • Harvest: Harvest flowers & leaves when flowering for best activity.

Lavender

  • Harvest: Harvest leaves in summer, roots in autumn (if making root tincture).
